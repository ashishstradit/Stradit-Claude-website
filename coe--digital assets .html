03Smart Contracting

Contracts that enforce compliance and automate lifecycle — without manual intervention.

Tokenized securities are only as trustworthy as the contracts governing them. Stradit designs, writes, formally verifies, and audits smart contracts that encode the regulatory, operational, and corporate action logic of institutional-grade securities — on any EVM-compatible or permissioned chain.

Tokenized security lifecycle End-to-end smart contract coverage: primary issuance, secondary transfer, dividend distribution, coupon payment, maturity, and redemption — fully automated.
Embedded KYC/AML compliance Transfer restrictions, investor whitelisting, and jurisdiction rules enforced at the contract layer — compliance logic that cannot be bypassed.
Corporate action automation Stock splits, tender offers, proxy voting, and rights entitlements processed automatically by the contract — eliminating the operational overhead of manual events.
Formal verification & audit Mathematical proof of contract correctness before deployment. Independent audit report and vulnerability assessment included in every engagement.
Multi-party execution & escrow Atomic multi-signature settlement between brokers, custodians, and counterparties — no single point of failure, no trust assumptions beyond the code.
